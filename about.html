About Us

SplitStream is a platform that allows users to share streaming platforms with others, providing a solution to situations where people share their login information. The website offers various sharing options, allowing users to share their streaming platforms with two, three, or four people, depending on the number of concurrent users allowed by the streaming platform. The website uses a sophisticated encryption system to safeguard user data and has an advanced security system in place designed to detect and prevent fraudulent activities. The website is designed to be user-friendly, with a search function, review system, and messaging system. SplitStream is committed to providing a comprehensive and customizable platform for sharing streaming services with others while protecting user data.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform that allows individuals to share streaming platforms with others, while also providing advanced security features to protect their personal information. Our website aims to provide a solution to situations where people share their login information, which goes against the terms of service of the streaming platforms. By allowing users to split the cost of a plan and grant them a user authentication code to access the account, our website aims to provide a cost-effective solution that benefits both users and streaming platform companies. We are also committed to reducing electronic waste and energy consumption by allowing more people to have access to these platforms at a discounted cost. Our goal is to provide users with a comprehensive and customizable platform for sharing streaming services with others, while also protecting their personal information and providing them with an enjoyable and secure streaming experience.

Vision

At SplitStream, we want everyone to have access to streaming platforms without breaking the bank. Our platform offers customizable sharing plans that allow users to split the cost of their favorite streaming services with friends or family. Sharing a platform not only reduces costs but also helps reduce electronic waste and energy consumption. Our advanced security features protect users' personal information and data. Our user-friendly platform features a robust search function and a review system to help users find and choose the right shared streaming platform. Our messaging system allows users to communicate and coordinate payments and plans. By sharing a platform, entertainment becomes accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. Our platform is a sustainable solution that benefits both users and the environment.
